## Problem

Reinsurance capital is locked behind opaque, relationship-driven markets accessible only to large institutional players, with high intermediation costs and no way for cedents or investors to independently audit counterparty exposure or track payout obligations in real time.

## Solution

A protocol enabling direct reinsurer participation in transparent risk pools — with tokenized risk tranches, automated payout distribution when loss events are verified, and onchain visibility into reserve levels and counterparty exposure that any participant can audit independently.

## Why Ethereum

Composability: risk pools are transparent and composable — any investor can participate and any insurer can access capacity without navigating opaque relationship-driven markets.
