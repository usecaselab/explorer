## Problem

Mortgage servicing and secondary market trading depend on legacy systems with slow reconciliation, making loan transfers error-prone and limiting borrower access to competitive terms.

## Solution

Servicing and secondary markets become programmable, traceable and accessible

## Why Ethereum

Composability: programmable mortgages can integrate with DeFi liquidity, automated servicing, and secondary trading — making a market that currently requires multiple intermediaries composable by default.
