## Problem

There is no widespread mechanism to verify who created a post, photo, video, or article — making impersonation trivial and deepfake attribution impossible to contest. The problem compounds with AI: AI-generated content is indistinguishable from human-created content once shared, and users receiving AI outputs have no way to verify which model produced a given result, enabling manipulation and undermining accountability for both human and AI-generated misinformation.

## Solution

Signed posts, media receipts, and provenance chains that cover both human-created and AI-generated content — cryptographically binding content to its origin at creation time, so that deepfakes, impersonation, and model-version manipulation are publicly detectable.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: cryptographic signatures attached at creation time prove who made content and when — impersonation and retroactive denial become publicly detectable rather than just deniable.
