## Problem

Verifying a company's registration, licensing status, or true ownership requires navigating multiple disconnected databases — and existing beneficial ownership registries rely on self-reported filings with no verification mechanism, allowing shell company structures to hide the real owners of assets across jurisdictions. This structural opacity is systematically exploited for money laundering, sanctions evasion, and tax fraud.

## Solution

Cryptographically attested business registration and beneficial ownership records that chain legal entities to verified ultimate owners across jurisdictions — enabling instant verification for regulators, banks, and counterparties without relying on self-filed declarations or bilateral data-sharing between registries.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: ownership attestations are cryptographically signed and immutable across jurisdictions — false filings become detectable and cross-border verification happens on a shared neutral ledger rather than through slow bilateral regulatory cooperation.
