## Problem

Royalty distribution in music, film, publishing, and patents passes through layers of opaque intermediaries who delay payments, misattribute rights, and make it nearly impossible for creators to audit what they're owed. Multi-party licensing agreements add further complexity — a single song can have dozens of co-writers, publishers, and performers each owed a different split, calculated manually and paid months after the usage occurred.

## Solution

Smart contracts that meter actual usage across media types and automatically distribute royalties to all rights holders at the moment of use — eliminating intermediary opacity, payment delays, and the manual calculation errors that compound across multi-party splits.

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: usage is metered and payments execute automatically at the moment of use — rights holders receive what they're owed without relying on intermediaries to calculate or remit correctly.
