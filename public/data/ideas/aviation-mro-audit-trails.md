## Problem

Airlines and MRO providers maintain aircraft maintenance records across disconnected paper logbooks and legacy systems, making FAA/EASA audit preparation weeks of manual work and leaving no reliable way to detect falsified inspection signoffs — a safety failure that has contributed to multiple airworthiness directives and enforcement actions.

## Solution

Immutable, per-component maintenance logs that record every inspection, part replacement, and airworthiness directive compliance event — giving regulators a continuous, tamper-evident audit trail rather than sampling paper records during periodic inspections.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: each maintenance event is cryptographically attested at the time it occurs — falsified inspections become detectable after the fact, and audit preparation collapses from weeks to hours because the regulator reads the same ledger the MRO writes to.
