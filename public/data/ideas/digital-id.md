## Problem

Citizens must re-verify identity separately for every government agency they interact with, while birth, death, marriage, and residency records sit in siloed paper-based systems that are vulnerable to loss and inconsistency across jurisdictions.

## Solution

A single, verifiable citizen identity that works across government services and agencies, with civil registry records stored in a tamper-evident, interoperable format.

## Why Ethereum

Composability: a single onchain identity credential works across any government service that accepts the standard — no rebuildling identity infrastructure for each agency.
