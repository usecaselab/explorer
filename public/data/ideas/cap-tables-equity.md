## Problem

Startup cap tables live in expensive, siloed platforms like Carta where ownership records can fall out of sync, transfers require manual legal work, and founders have no real-time, verifiable source of truth.

## Solution

Verifiable, onchain ownership records from day one (issuance, vesting, transfers)

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: ownership records are cryptographically signed and publicly auditable, so there's a single source of truth that can't silently diverge between parties or be altered without detection.
