## Problem

Permit applications — for water connections, construction, business licenses — pass through multiple agencies via disconnected workflows where records are mutable, approvals are opaque, and the process is vulnerable to favoritism and corruption. Applicants have no independent way to verify that their application was assessed on its merits.

## Solution

Immutable, publicly auditable records of permit applications, approvals, fee payments, and decision rationale — with community oversight built in — so that every step of the permitting process is verifiable and deviations from standard procedure are detectable.

## Why Ethereum

Composability: permit workflows that reference shared identity and document standards can cross agency boundaries without custom integrations — reducing the handoff gaps that cause months-long delays.
