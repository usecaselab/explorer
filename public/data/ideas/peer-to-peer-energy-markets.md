## Problem

Rooftop solar owners and other distributed generators have no direct way to sell surplus electricity to their neighbors, forced instead to feed it back to the grid at wholesale rates set by monopoly utilities.

## Solution

Local energy trading and microgrids that let participants buy, sell, and balance electricity directly with one another

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: energy trades settle automatically when smart meters confirm delivery — no utility intermediary required to match buyers and sellers or process the transaction.
