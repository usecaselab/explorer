## Problem

Ad networks report impressions and clicks using their own unauditable metrics, enabling widespread fraud. Brands also pay influencers based on self-reported or platform-reported reach and engagement that is trivially inflated through bots and fake followers.

## Solution

Transparent impression tracking and verifiable reach metrics — with optional zero-knowledge proofs for audience size — so advertisers can independently confirm that spend reached real human audiences.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: delivery events and engagement signals can be cryptographically attested — giving advertisers independent proof that their spend reached real audiences without relying on the platform's own reporting.
