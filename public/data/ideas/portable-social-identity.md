## Problem

Users who build audiences on one social platform lose everything if they switch apps or get de-platformed. Follower counts and engagement metrics are also easily manipulated and impossible to verify independently.

## Solution

Onchain handles, profiles, and social graphs that work across multiple apps — combined with verifiable attestations for engagement and community participation that users own and carry with them.

## Why Ethereum

Composability: an onchain identity and social graph are readable by any application — users switch platforms without losing followers, and reputation is verifiable rather than gameable.
