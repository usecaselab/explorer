## Problem

Open-source contributors build critical infrastructure but have no portable record of their contributions, and funding decisions are made by a small number of gatekeepers disconnected from the people doing the work.

## Solution

P2P versioning and funding for open source, with contributor credentials, governance tokens and transparent reward flows

## Why Ethereum

Composability: contribution records and governance tokens live on a shared layer — any funding platform or organization can recognize and reward the same verified contribution history.
