## Problem

SMEs extend trade credit to each other in isolated bilateral relationships, missing opportunities to net circular obligations and free up working capital that is locked in receivables chains.

## Solution

Netting obligations across SME networks

## Why Ethereum

Composability: receivables across a network can be atomically netted in a single transaction — something impossible to coordinate bilaterally but trivial when all obligations live on a shared ledger.
