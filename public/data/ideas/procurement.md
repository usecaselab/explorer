## Problem

Purchase orders, vendor agreements, and replenishment workflows run through email chains and disconnected ERP modules, causing delays, duplicate orders, and disputes over terms that were never machine-readable.

## Solution

Smart contracts for purchase orders, RFQs, RFPs, vendor agreements, or automatic replenishment plans

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: purchase order terms, vendor agreements, and replenishment triggers become machine-readable and self-executing, removing email chains and ERP reconciliation from the critical path.
