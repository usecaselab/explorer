## Problem

Scientific knowledge is fragmented across millions of papers, databases, and disciplines with no interoperable layer connecting related findings, making cross-domain discovery largely accidental.

## Solution

Decentralized knowledge graphs linking data, AI, and researchers to reveal hidden connections

## Why Ethereum

Composability: a shared onchain knowledge layer lets any application connect related findings across institutions and disciplines — without bilateral data-sharing agreements between research databases.
