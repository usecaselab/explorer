## Problem

Any digital system that gives one vote, one reward, or one allocation per person is immediately gamed by whoever can spin up the most fake accounts — airdrops get drained by bots, governance votes get bought in bulk, and social platforms are flooded with coordinated inauthentic behavior — because there is no cheap, privacy-preserving way to prove an account belongs to a unique real human.

## Solution

Systems that establish a cryptographic guarantee that an account belongs to a unique living person — without revealing who that person is — enabling sybil-resistant token distributions, one-person-one-vote governance, and spam-free social platforms.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: personhood attestations are cryptographically issued and publicly checkable by any application — platforms verify uniqueness without storing biometric data or trusting a single identity provider that becomes a centralization point.
