## Problem

Creators proving when a work was made and inventors establishing priority in patent disputes both rely on slow, expensive government registries that vary by jurisdiction and are vulnerable to retroactive challenge. Inventors lack a tamper-proof way to timestamp lab notebooks and experimental data, while creators have no instant, cross-jurisdictional proof of authorship that doesn't require navigating multiple national systems.

## Solution

Immutable, cryptographically timestamped records for both creative works and inventions — establishing proof of creation, authorship, and prior art at the moment of registration, verifiable instantly across jurisdictions without filing separately in each one.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: a cryptographic timestamp on an immutable ledger proves exactly when a work existed and who registered it — creating evidence of ownership that any court or platform can verify without a government registry.
