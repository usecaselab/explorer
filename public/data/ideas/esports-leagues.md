## Problem

Esports tournaments rely on centralized organizers for brackets, anti-cheat enforcement, and prize distribution, introducing disputes over fairness, delayed payouts, and opaque decision-making.

## Solution

Transparent tournament brackets, provable anti-cheat attestations, instant rule-based payouts reduce overhead and disputes

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: prize pools are held in smart contracts and released automatically based on verifiable match results — removing tournament operators as a trusted intermediary for payout and bracket management.
