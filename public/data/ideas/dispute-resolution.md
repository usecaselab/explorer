## Problem

Resolving disputes in B2B contracts and freelance agreements requires expensive, slow litigation or arbitrary platform support teams that apply opaque rules — pricing out individuals and small businesses from meaningful access to justice and leaving the outcome entirely in the hands of a platform that has its own interests.

## Solution

Decentralized arbitration mechanisms where specialized juror pools evaluate disputes using standardized evidence and structured deliberation — with jurors staking tokens on verdicts, outcomes enforced automatically via smart contract, and no ability for a losing party to delay compliance.

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: arbitration outcomes execute automatically via smart contract — no separate enforcement step, and no ability for a losing party to simply ignore the verdict or drag out compliance.
