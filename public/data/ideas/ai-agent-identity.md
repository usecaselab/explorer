## Problem

Autonomous AI agents and IoT devices interact with services and execute transactions but have no standardized identity, wallet, or payment rails — making it impossible to attribute actions, manage permissions, hold assets, or enable machine-scale micropayments without routing every transaction through human-operated intermediaries. IoT devices face the same gap at the infrastructure level: they depend on centralized certificate authorities for identity, creating single points of failure and making ownership transfer cumbersome.

## Solution

Onchain identity and payment rails purpose-built for autonomous agents and devices — enabling them to hold funds, pay for services, sign transactions, transfer ownership, and build verifiable track records across platforms without human approval loops or centralized registries.

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: smart contracts can scope an agent's permissions and spending limits programmatically, and Ethereum's global settlement layer means an agent's identity and funds work across any application without custom integrations.
