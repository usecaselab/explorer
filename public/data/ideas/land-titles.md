## Problem

Property ownership records are fragmented across county offices and paper files, making title searches slow, lien verification unreliable, and transfer histories easy to tamper with.

## Solution

Immutable land ownership records with lien checks and transparent transfer histories

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: ownership history is recorded as an immutable chain — making title fraud and unauthorized transfers publicly detectable without a trusted central registry.
