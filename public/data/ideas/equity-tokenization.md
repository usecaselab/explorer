## Problem

Going public through an IPO costs millions in legal, banking, and compliance fees, effectively locking out small and mid-sized companies from accessing broad equity markets.

## Solution

Issuing tokenized shares, bypassing expensive IPOs and private placements

## Why Ethereum

Composability: tokenized shares trade on open markets and integrate with DeFi lending, secondary trading, and governance tools without requiring a dedicated exchange or investment bank.
