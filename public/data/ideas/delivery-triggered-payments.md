## Problem

Suppliers and freight carriers wait weeks for payment after goods are delivered because reconciliation between shipping documents, invoices, and proof of delivery is manual, while carriers lack verifiable proof-of-location or proof-of-condition to trigger automatic settlement.

## Solution

Conditional release of funds tied to oracle-verified delivery events, proof-of-location, and proof-of-condition, automating settlement for both goods delivery and freight logistics

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: payment releases automatically when oracle-verified delivery events are confirmed — carriers and suppliers get paid the moment delivery is proven, not weeks later after manual reconciliation.
