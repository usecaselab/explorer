## Problem

Intellectual property rights are indivisible and illiquid, making it nearly impossible for small investors to participate in IP financing, for researchers to crowdfund early-stage IP, or for inventors to unlock the dormant value of their portfolios.

## Solution

Fractional ownership, crowdfunding, financing, and trading of intellectual property rights through IP-NFTs and tokenized IP instruments

## Why Ethereum

Composability: tokenized IP rights trade on open markets and can serve as collateral in lending protocols — unlocking liquidity from assets that are otherwise indivisible and untradeable.
