## Problem

In drought-prone regions, water rights are traded in informal bilateral markets with no price transparency, while actual usage is monitored by infrequent physical inspections that miss illegal diversions — creating a tragedy of the commons where overuse is profitable and nearly undetectable until aquifers are depleted.

## Solution

Tokenized water rights tradeable on transparent markets, with IoT-verified usage continuously recorded onchain — creating a real-time allocation registry where overuse is immediately visible and rights can be transferred without paper title processes.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: actual water usage attested by IoT sensors is recorded immutably — illegal diversions become publicly detectable as deviations from onchain allocations, rather than discovered only when downstream users notice reduced flow.
