## Problem

Employee and contractor permissions across company tools, bank accounts, and signing authorities are managed through disconnected admin panels, creating security gaps and making it impossible to enforce policies programmatically.

## Solution

Programmable, wallet-based authority systems enforcing who can move funds or sign agreements; onchain identifiers to represent employees/members

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: spending authorities and signing permissions are encoded in smart contracts and enforced programmatically — not dependent on each SaaS tool implementing policy correctly.
