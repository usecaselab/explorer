## Problem

Users who want to prove claims about their income, identity, or activity from existing platforms must either share raw credentials or rely on platform-issued APIs that can be revoked at will.

## Solution

Users prove claims from existing web platforms—income, identity, activity—for use in onchain applications, without requiring platform permission or surrendering raw data

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: zero-knowledge proofs let users cryptographically attest to data from any website — income, identity, activity — without requiring platform permission or exposing underlying raw data.
