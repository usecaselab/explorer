## Problem

Cross-border payroll and contractor payments route through multiple intermediaries, taking days to settle with high fees, while tax reporting and compliance for global teams remain a manual reconciliation nightmare.

## Solution

Instant, programmable global payouts with automated tax/KYC reporting

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: payment logic executes automatically on schedule via smart contract, and stablecoins settle instantly across borders without correspondent banking intermediaries.
