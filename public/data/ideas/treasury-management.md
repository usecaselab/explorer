## Problem

Company treasuries sit in traditional bank accounts earning minimal yield, with no programmable controls for spend limits, automated reporting, or real-time visibility into cash positions across entities and currencies.

## Solution

Stablecoin-based treasury with yield strategies, automated reporting, and compliance hooks

## Why Ethereum

Composability: a stablecoin treasury can plug directly into DeFi yield protocols, automated reporting tools, and multi-sig controls without bespoke integrations — the whole stack is interoperable by default.
