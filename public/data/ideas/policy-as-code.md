## Problem

Statutes and regulations are published as natural-language prose that requires expensive legal interpretation, making it impossible to automatically test whether a system or process is compliant before deployment. Enforcement compounds the problem: even where rules are clear, triggering consequences — traffic restrictions, fines, subsidy releases — requires human sign-off at each step, meaning enforcement reliably lags the events it's supposed to address.

## Solution

Machine-readable statutes and regulations with testable compliance logic — so businesses can verify conformance before deployment and enforcement responses execute automatically when oracle-verified conditions are met. Air quality policy is an immediate application: when sensor feeds cross legal thresholds, traffic restrictions and fines trigger without a human decision at each step.

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: machine-readable regulations can be directly referenced by smart contracts — compliance becomes testable before the fact and enforcement executes automatically, making the gap between a rule and its consequence a protocol property rather than a bureaucratic one.
