## Problem

Cross-border shipments are delayed at customs because clearance documents are exchanged via email and fax between agencies that have no shared, real-time view of compliance status.

## Solution

Shared ledgers for real-time clearance, tariff mechanics and document exchange between jurisdictions

## Why Ethereum

Composability: a shared compliance layer means customs documents are readable by agencies across jurisdictions without bilateral integrations — clearing happens in real time instead of through email and fax.
