## Problem

Platform revenue-sharing models take 30-50% of creator earnings, change terms unilaterally, and delay payouts, while creators have no native way to offer tiered access or token-based benefits independent of platform control.

## Solution

Subscriptions, tipping, revenue splits, micro-payments, gated access, and token-based community benefits embedded in creator-owned platforms

## Why Ethereum

Composability: subscriptions, tipping, and gated access built on a shared layer work across any app — creators aren't locked into one platform's monetization terms or payment schedules.
