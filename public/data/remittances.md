## Problem

A Kenyan nurse working in London sending $200 home loses $12–20 to Western Union and correspondent bank fees — more than the entire daily wage of the family member receiving it — while the transfer takes 1–3 days and requires both parties to have access to physical agents.

## Solution

Stablecoin transfers that move value internationally in minutes at near-zero marginal cost, with recipients cashing out through local mobile money networks or holding value in digital wallets without needing a bank account.

## Why Ethereum

Composability: stablecoins settle on a shared global layer without correspondent banking infrastructure — sender and recipient transact directly regardless of jurisdiction, with no intermediary banks extracting spread on each hop.
