## Problem

People's personal data is scattered across hundreds of apps and services that collect it without meaningful consent, offering no way for individuals to control access, revoke sharing, or capture value from their own information.

## Solution

User-controlled data stores that allow granular consent, selective disclosure, and optional monetization without surrendering raw data

## Why Ethereum

Composability: a single onchain identity can attach granular consent permissions to any application — one vault that works across the ecosystem rather than re-authorizing each platform separately.
