## Problem

Gun ownership records are either nonexistent, fragmented across jurisdictions, or stored in systems that cannot balance public-safety traceability with the privacy rights of lawful owners.

## Solution

Encrypted, privacy-preserving registries to enable lawful ownership and traceability

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: cryptographic ownership records prove provenance without exposing owner identity publicly — selective disclosure lets authorized parties verify while preserving privacy for lawful owners.
