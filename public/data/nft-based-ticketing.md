## Problem

Event ticketing is plagued by scalper bots, counterfeit tickets, and opaque resale markets where venues and artists lose control over pricing and capture none of the secondary-market value.

## Solution

Verifiable, resalable access to events, concerts, and festivals

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: resale price caps and royalty splits execute automatically at point of transfer — venues and artists capture secondary market value and retain control over pricing without a centralized ticket platform.
