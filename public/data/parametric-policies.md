## Problem

Traditional insurance claims require lengthy manual verification and adjudication, delaying payouts for weeks or months after weather events, flight cancellations, or cargo losses when policyholders need funds most.

## Solution

Smart-contract triggered claims (weather, flight delay, cargo) eliminate long verification loops

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: smart contracts trigger payouts automatically when oracle-verified events occur — no adjuster, no claim form, no waiting period.
