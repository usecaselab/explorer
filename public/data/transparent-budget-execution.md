## Problem

Public funds flow through opaque, fragmented ledgers between appropriation and expenditure, making it nearly impossible for citizens or auditors to trace how tax revenue is actually spent.

## Solution

Real-time tracking of public funds from appropriation to expenditure

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: every appropriation and expenditure is recorded on a public ledger — making it possible to trace how tax revenue flows from allocation to final use, rather than disappearing into opaque departmental accounts.
