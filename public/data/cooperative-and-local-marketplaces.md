## Problem

Small producers selling through conventional distribution channels lose most of their margin to intermediaries, while consumers who want to buy local have no transparent, community-governed platform to connect with them directly.

## Solution

Community-owned platforms that let producers sell directly and keep more economic value within local food systems

## Why Ethereum

Composability: a shared marketplace layer means any producer can participate without platform lock-in, and payments, reviews, and provenance data interoperate across the ecosystem.
