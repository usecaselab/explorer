## Problem

Emissions caps, fishing quotas, and biodiversity credits are tracked through incompatible national systems that cannot be independently verified, enabling widespread overuse of shared natural resources.

## Solution

Verifiable tracking of emissions, fishing quotas, biodiversity credits or water-basin usage across borders

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: resource usage data from multiple jurisdictions is recorded on a neutral shared ledger — making it possible to detect overuse without relying on any single country's self-reporting.
