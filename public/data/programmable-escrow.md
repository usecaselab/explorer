## Problem

Payment disputes between service providers and clients are resolved by platform operators with opaque rules, and milestone-based payments require manual verification that both parties can contest.

## Solution

Smart contracts for milestone-based payments, dispute resolution, and refunds that minimize platform mediation

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: payment releases when verifiable milestones are confirmed — neither party can stall or dispute arbitrarily, and no platform operator needs to mediate.
