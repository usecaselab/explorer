## Problem

Product reviews are easily faked and purchase receipts are siloed within individual retailers, giving consumers no reliable way to verify that a reviewer actually bought the product.

## Solution

Onchain attestations improve authenticity, support returns and reduce dispute risk

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: purchase receipts are cryptographically attested — platforms can verify that a reviewer actually bought the product without requiring access to proprietary transaction records.
