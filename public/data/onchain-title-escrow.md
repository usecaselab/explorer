## Problem

Real estate closings take 30-60 days and require title companies, notaries, and attorneys to manually verify ownership chains in paper registries, adding thousands of dollars in fees to every transaction.

## Solution

Smart contracts replace paper registries and manual escrow flows, reducing settlement time and improving transparency

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: title transfer and escrow release execute automatically when conditions are verified — removing the 30–60 day closing process that currently requires title companies, notaries, and attorneys.
