## Problem

Insurance policies are illiquid, non-transferable instruments that policyholders cannot sell, swap, or restructure if their risk profile changes, trapping capital and limiting flexibility.

## Solution

Policies become tradable contracts, enabling secondary markets, swapping risk profiles and unlocking liquidity

## Why Ethereum

Composability: tokenized policies trade on open markets and can be bundled, split, or collateralized — unlocking liquidity that paper policies permanently forfeit.
