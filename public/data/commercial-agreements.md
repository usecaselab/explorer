## Problem

Business contracts are static PDFs disconnected from actual execution, so milestones, royalty calculations, volume discounts, and dispute resolution all require manual tracking and expensive legal intervention.

## Solution

Legal smart contracts embedding milestones, royalties, licensing, rebates, volume discounts, performance incentives, warranties, expiries, dispute handling directly into execution logic

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: milestones, royalties, volume discounts, and dispute terms are encoded directly into the agreement and execute automatically — removing the manual tracking and legal intervention that make contracts expensive to administer.
