## Problem

Carbon credits and biodiversity offsets are traded as undifferentiated commodities despite vast differences in methodology and verification rigor, allowing low-quality credits to crowd out legitimate ones. Forests, watersheds, and biodiversity also lack liquid market representations.

## Solution

Differentiated environmental asset classes with onchain provenance, methodology metadata, and verification quality — enabling transparent pricing of ecological assets and markets that reward genuine impact.

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: provenance and methodology are cryptographically attached to each credit — buyers can verify quality without relying on opaque rating agencies, and double-counting is publicly detectable.
