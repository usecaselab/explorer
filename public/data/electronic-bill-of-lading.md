## Problem

The bill of lading, the single most important document in international shipping, still circulates as a paper original that must be physically couriered between parties, causing delays and fraud risk.

## Solution

Replacing paper document of title with NFT-like digital representation of title and transfer rights

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: title and transfer rights execute onchain — the document of title is the asset itself, eliminating the physical courier step and the fraud it enables.
