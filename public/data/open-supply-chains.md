## Problem

Independent farmers and artisans are excluded from global markets because supply-chain coordination tools are built for large corporations and require expensive integrations they cannot afford.

## Solution

Enabling coordination among independent farmers, artisans and open-hardware producers outside corporate platforms

## Why Ethereum

Composability: open supply-chain standards mean any producer can plug in without proprietary integrations — coordination happens on a shared layer accessible to small players and large corporations equally.
