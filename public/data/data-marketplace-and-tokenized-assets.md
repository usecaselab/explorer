## Problem

Data trades through opaque brokers and bespoke NDA-driven deals where buyers can't verify quality or provenance, while valuable datasets and analytics outputs have no standard way to be priced, composed, or traded like financial instruments.

## Solution

Platforms where data access is governed by smart contracts — enabling licensing, metering, revocation, royalties, and automatic revenue splits — with data exposed as onchain assets that can be priced, combined, and traded

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: licensing terms, usage metering, and revenue splits execute automatically — data producers earn royalties proportional to actual consumption without relying on a broker to report usage honestly.
