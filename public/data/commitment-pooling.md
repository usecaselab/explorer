## Problem

Small businesses, cooperatives, and credit unions lack mechanisms to pool reserves and share liquidity risk without expensive legacy banking infrastructure, forcing each member to maintain costly individual cash buffers.

## Solution

Blockchain-based cooperative finance that lets members pool capital, share liquidity, and access credit from one another — governed transparently and auditable by all participants.

## Why Ethereum

Composability: pooled capital is accessible to all members through shared smart contracts, and the ledger provides a transparent audit trail that replaces the legacy banking infrastructure these organizations currently depend on.
