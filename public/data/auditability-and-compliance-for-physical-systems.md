## Problem

Physical systems in manufacturing, logistics, and infrastructure generate safety and maintenance data that is stored in mutable, disconnected databases, making regulatory audits unreliable and tampering undetectable.

## Solution

Tamper-evident logs linking physical events to digital records for safety, maintenance, and regulatory reporting

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: physical events are logged as immutable onchain records — making tampering after the fact detectable and giving regulators a trustworthy audit trail they didn't have to create themselves.
