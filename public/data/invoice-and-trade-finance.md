## Problem

Small businesses and exporters wait 30-90 days for payment on legitimate invoices and receivables, while factoring intermediaries charge steep discount rates and trade finance lenders reject applications because documents are unverifiable PDFs prone to double-pledging.

## Solution

Tokenized invoices, receivables, and letters of credit that can be verified, sold, collateralized, or traded in real-time

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: tokenized invoices carry a cryptographic chain of ownership that prevents double-pledging and allows any lender to independently verify authenticity — the core fraud vector in traditional invoice financing.
