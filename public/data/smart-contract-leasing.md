## Problem

Lease management involves manual rent collection, opaque deposit handling, and paper-based renewals that create disputes between landlords and tenants with no neutral enforcement mechanism.

## Solution

Lease payments, deposits, renewals and dispute resolution run via code, eliminating many intermediaries

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: rent payments, deposit holds, and renewal terms execute automatically via smart contract — neither party can unilaterally alter terms, and disputes resolve against pre-agreed code.
