## Problem

Issuing and settling government debt involves layers of intermediaries, T+2 or longer settlement windows, and high minimum denominations that exclude smaller investors from participating.

## Solution

Onchain issuance, settlement, and coupon tracking of public debt instruments, enabling faster settlement, lower costs, and broader investor access

## Why Ethereum

Composability: tokenized bonds settle instantly, pay coupons automatically, and can be held directly by retail investors — removing intermediary layers and T+2 settlement delays.
