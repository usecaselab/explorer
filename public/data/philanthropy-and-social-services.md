## Ideas

### Direct Cash Transfers
Crypto donations sent directly to the wallets of people in need, bypassing NGOs and administrative fees.

### Conditional Giving
Funds that are only released when verifiable milestones (e.g., building complete, trees planted) are met.

### Impact Certificates
NFTs that represent a verifiable unit of positive impact (e.g., 1 ton of carbon removed), sold to funders.
