## Problem

Revenue sharing among partners, investors, and employees requires manual calculation and distribution each period, introducing delays, errors, and disputes that compound as organizations scale.

## Solution

Automated distributions to partners, employees, and investors tied to income events; tokenized revenue/profits; tokenize receivables/payables

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: revenue flows are split and distributed automatically at the moment of receipt according to rules set once — no manual calculation, no payment delays, no disputes about the math.
