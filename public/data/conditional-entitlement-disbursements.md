## Problem

Benefit and subsidy payments rely on manual eligibility checks that are slow, error-prone, and vulnerable to fraud, with no programmatic link between verified conditions and fund release.

## Solution

Subsidy and benefit payments that execute automatically against verifiable eligibility criteria

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: benefit payments execute automatically when eligibility criteria are met — removing manual verification steps that introduce delays and fraud vulnerability.
