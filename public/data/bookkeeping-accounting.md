## Problem

Double-entry bookkeeping produces records that each party maintains independently, making audits slow and expensive because there is no shared, tamper-evident ledger that all counterparties can trust.

## Solution

Real-time, tamper-evident “triple-entry” ledgers providing auditable financial history

## Why Ethereum

Verifiability: every transaction is recorded on a shared, tamper-evident ledger that all counterparties can independently audit — eliminating the reconciliation step that makes audits slow and expensive.
