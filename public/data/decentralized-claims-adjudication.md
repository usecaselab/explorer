title: "Decentralized insurance claims adjudication"
domains: insurance, health

## Problem

Insurance companies face hundreds of disputed claims monthly that don't fit automated processing rules — ambiguous coverage questions, contested damage assessments, conflicting evidence — and route them through traditional arbitration that takes 7–10 days, costs hundreds of dollars per case, and produces outcomes neither party finds credible.

## Solution

Specialized juror pools that evaluate disputed insurance claims using standardized evidence packages and structured deliberation protocols — with jurors staking tokens on their verdicts, outcomes determined by stake-weighted consensus, and payment executing automatically once adjudication is complete.

## Why Ethereum

Enforcement: adjudication outcomes execute automatically via smart contract — neither the insurer nor the claimant can delay compliance with a verdict, and the juror incentive structure is enforced programmatically rather than by a professional arbitration body.
