Ethereum is powerful but underutilized

DeFi and stablecoins already settle trillions in value but these are just the first building blocks of the broader internet of value.

The bigger picture is self-sovereign coordination

Ethereum is designed to enable unstoppable money, organizations, markets; user-owned identity, agents, social networks; programmable governance, insurance, credit, property rights.

Focusing on what Ethereum uniquely unlocks

The Use Case Lab starts from specific user problems that Ethereum can solve and works backward to define the breakthroughs required to unlock these solutions.

Working hands-on to remove blockers

We collaborate with builders, companies, and institutions to overcome critical blockers holding back high-potential use cases.

Aligning the ecosystem around a shared vision

Join us in defining the use cases that will unshackle the world from trusted intermediaries.
